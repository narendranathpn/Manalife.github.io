About Us

Mission

At Manalife AI, our mission is to transform maternity and women’s healthcare through intelligent and accessible technology. We empower gynaecologists, healthcare providers, and expectant mothers with AI-driven insights that support early detection, personalized care, and improved clinical outcomes. A key focus of our work is enabling early screening and intervention for cervical cancer, addressing a critical gap in preventive care—especially in underserved regions.

Team

The Manalife founding team brings over 100+ years of combined experience across technology, healthcare operations, gynaecology, and oncology. This diverse expertise allows us to build solutions that are both clinically grounded and technologically advanced. We are supported by a global panel of advisors in medicine, medical technology, and regulatory strategy, ensuring our work remains relevant, evidence-based, and globally compliant.

Geographies

We are currently serving gynaecologists and expectant mothers in India and Malaysia, two regions with diverse healthcare landscapes and growing demand for digital innovation in maternal care. In India, we focus on bridging gaps in both urban and rural settings, where access to timely diagnostics and personalized care remains a challenge. In Malaysia, we collaborate with clinics and health systems to support national efforts in digital health transformation, including the shift from paper-based maternity records to intelligent, AI-powered platforms. Our goal is to expand thoughtfully, building on local insights while delivering globally scalable solutions.

Solutions

Manalife Core

Manalife Core is the foundation of our digital maternity care platform, built to streamline and elevate the daily workflows of gynaecologists, clinics, and hospitals. It features a gynaecology-specific Electronic Medical Record (EMR) system inspired from Redbook, along with tools for appointment scheduling, video consultations, and integration with home-based diagnostic devices. The platform also includes a companion app for expectant mothers, enabling them to journal their daily experiences, complete check-ins with their doctors, and access curated educational content. Designed with a strong emphasis on usability and clinical relevance, Manalife Core empowers care providers to deliver more efficient, connected, and personalized maternity care.

Manalife AI

Manalife AI is the intelligence layer of the platform, designed to support clinical decision-making and improve maternal health outcomes through data-driven insights. It leverages machine learning models to analyze patient data, identify potential risks, and generate timely alerts for gynaecologists. Key applications include predictive monitoring during antenatal care, automated triaging, and personalized care pathways. Built with explainability and clinician trust in mind, Manalife AI transforms routine care into proactive, precision-driven healthcare.

Clinical Decision Support for Gynaecologists

Manalife AI provides gynaecologists with real-time, context-aware decision support that enhances clinical accuracy and response time. By continuously analyzing patient data—including vitals, symptoms, lab results, and scan reports—the system flags emerging risks such as preeclampsia, gestational diabetes, and fetal growth anomalies. Clinicians receive evidence-backed recommendations that are integrated into their workflow, ensuring timely and informed interventions without added cognitive load.

Cervical Cancer Detection

A flagship capability of Manalife AI is its AI-powered cervical cancer screening tool, which assists clinicians in early detection of pre-cancerous and cancerous lesions from colposcopy or cytology images. The system leverages advanced image processing and deep learning algorithms to highlight suspicious regions and assign risk levels. Designed to augment clinical judgment, this tool improves diagnostic accuracy and enables timely referrals and treatment, especially in low-resource settings where specialist availability may be limited.
